About Our Factory

25+ years of expertise in sunglasses manufacturing

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Guangwei Optics has grown from a small workshop to one of China's leading sunglasses manufacturers. With over 25 years of experience, we serve brands, wholesalers, and retailers in 30+ countries.

Our 8,000 square meter facility in Zhejiang province houses 5 automated production lines, a dedicated R&D center with 50+ engineers, and a rigorous 3-stage quality control system. From classic aviator styles to high-end luxury frames, we have the expertise and capacity to deliver at any scale.
